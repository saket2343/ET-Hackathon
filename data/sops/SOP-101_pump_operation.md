# SOP-101 — Normal Operation of Centrifugal Pump P-101

## 1. Start-Up
1. Confirm suction valve V-101 fully open and discharge valve V-102 cracked open.
2. Vent the casing until liquid appears at the vent.
3. Start motor M-101 from HS-101; watch discharge pressure PI-101 rise to
   6.5 bar nominal.
4. Slowly open V-102 to the operating position. Never run the pump against a
   closed discharge valve for more than 60 seconds.

## 2. Normal Operating Limits
- Discharge pressure PI-101: 6.0–7.0 bar
- Bearing vibration VT-101: below 2.8 mm/s RMS
- Bearing temperature: below 75 °C
- Flow FI-101: 40–55 m³/h

## 3. Shutdown
Close V-102 to minimum flow, stop M-101, then close V-101 if the pump is to be
isolated.
