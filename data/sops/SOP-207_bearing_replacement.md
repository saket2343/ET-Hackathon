# SOP-207 — Bearing Replacement, Centrifugal Pump P-101

## 1. Scope
Replacement of the drive-end and non-drive-end bearings (part BRG-204) on
centrifugal pump P-101.

## 2. Prerequisites
- LOTO isolation completed per SAF-12 Section 3 (permit REQUIRED).
- Spare bearing BRG-204 (SKF 6310-2Z equivalent) drawn from stores.
- Torque wrench calibrated within the last 12 months.

## 3. Removal
1. Remove coupling guard and disconnect coupling from motor M-101.
2. Drain bearing housing oil into a labelled container.
3. Remove bearing housing end covers.
4. Pull both bearings with a mechanical puller. Never reuse a pulled bearing.

## 4. Installation
1. Heat the new BRG-204 bearing to 110 °C maximum using an induction heater.
2. Install on shaft with marked face outboard.
3. Torque the bearing housing cover bolts to 45 N·m in a star pattern.
4. Fill the bearing housing with ISO VG 68 oil to the sight-glass centreline
   (approximately 0.9 L).

## 5. Alignment
After coupling reconnection, perform laser shaft alignment. Maximum allowable
offset is 0.05 mm; maximum angularity 0.04 mm/100 mm. Misalignment is the most
common cause of premature bearing failure on this pump.

## 6. Post-Maintenance Test
Run pump for 30 minutes. Vibration velocity at the drive-end bearing must not
exceed 2.8 mm/s RMS. Record readings in the CMMS work order.
