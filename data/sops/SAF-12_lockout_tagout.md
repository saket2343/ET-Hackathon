# SAF-12 — Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) Procedure for Rotating Equipment

## 1. Purpose
This procedure defines the mandatory energy isolation steps required before any
maintenance activity on rotating equipment in Area 1, including centrifugal pumps
P-101 and P-102 and drive motor M-101.

## 2. When LOTO Is Required
A LOTO permit is REQUIRED before any of the following activities:
- Bearing replacement or inspection on pump P-101 or motor M-101
- Coupling alignment or removal
- Seal replacement
- Any work that requires guard removal on rotating equipment

## 3. Isolation Steps (Section 3)
1. Notify the control room and obtain LOTO permit number.
2. Stop pump P-101 from the local control station HS-101.
3. Open motor breaker MCC-1-04 for M-101 and lock in the OPEN position.
4. Close suction valve V-101 and discharge valve V-102; lock and tag both valves.
5. Vent trapped pressure through drain valve V-103 to the closed drain header.
6. Verify zero energy: attempt local start, confirm no rotation, confirm 0 psi
   on PI-101.
7. Each worker applies a personal lock and tag before starting work.

## 4. Restoration
Remove locks in reverse order. Only the worker who applied a lock may remove it.
The permit is closed by the shift supervisor after a walk-down.
