# Vendor Manual — Centrifugal Pump CX-200 (installed as P-101)

## 1. Equipment Data
- Type: single-stage end-suction centrifugal pump, API-610 OH1
- Rated flow: 50 m³/h at 65 m head, 2950 rpm
- Driver: 37 kW induction motor (site tag M-101) via flexible coupling
- Bearings: two deep-groove ball bearings, part BRG-204 (SKF 6310-2Z equivalent)
- Lubrication: oil bath, ISO VG 68, 0.9 L capacity

## 2. Bearing Life and Failure Modes
Design L10 bearing life is 40,000 hours under rated conditions. The dominant
failure modes observed on the CX-200 fleet are:
1. Misalignment-induced bearing wear — presents as steadily rising 1x vibration
   over days to weeks.
2. Lubrication degradation — presents as bearing temperature rise above 85 °C.
3. Cavitation damage — presents as broadband noise and fluctuating discharge
   pressure.

A rising vibration trend at the drive-end bearing with stable temperature is
characteristic of failure mode 1 and warrants bearing replacement and laser
alignment check.

## 3. Key Torque Values
- Bearing housing cover bolts: 45 N·m
- Coupling hub set screws: 25 N·m
- Casing drain plug: 30 N·m

## 4. Spares
Recommended on-site spares: 2 × bearing BRG-204, 1 × mechanical seal MS-88,
1 × coupling element CE-31.
